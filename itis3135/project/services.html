Our Services

At Clifford Favors Consulting, we offer personalized insurance and risk management services for companies and professionals looking to strengthen their financial resilience. Explore our key offerings below.

Corporate Risk Assessment

We analyze your entire risk portfolio to identify potential threats and propose cost-effective strategies to minimize liabilities. From operational risks to regulatory exposure, our evaluations are comprehensive and tailored to your business structure.

Insurance Policy Review & Optimization

We dive into your existing policies to detect coverage gaps, overlaps, or unnecessary expenses. Our team works to renegotiate terms with your providers or recommend better-fit alternatives.

Consulting for Mergers & Acquisitions

Transitioning during M&A events can introduce new liabilities. Clifford Favors helps ensure your insurance and risk frameworks are adaptive and scalable throughout the process.

Executive Liability Management

Protecting leadership is essential. We provide customized solutions for D&O insurance, EPLI, and fiduciary liability to safeguard your C-suite and board members from legal claims.

Cyber Risk Consulting

As cyber threats continue to evolve, we help clients assess digital exposure and craft strategic cyber insurance and mitigation plans aligned with their IT infrastructure and compliance needs.
