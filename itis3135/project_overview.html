Project Overview

Overview

Application Description and Purpose:

This web application will serve as an informational and promotional platform for Clifford Favors, Vice President at Lockton. The goal is to showcase his industry experience, professional services, and serve as a digital touchpoint for those seeking expert insurance and risk management consulting services.

Intended Users:

The intended audience includes corporate clients, insurance professionals, potential collaborators, and individuals seeking consultation on complex insurance strategies. This platform is meant to appeal to both established clients and newcomers to the industry.

Content:

The site will feature detailed descriptions of services, a biography highlighting Clifford Favors' education and professional journey, client success stories, contact forms, a calendar for consultations, and informative industry insights or news updates.
